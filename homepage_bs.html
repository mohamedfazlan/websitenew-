Our Mission

At our bakery, we want to make really good meals that everyone can afford. We care a lot about our customers, especially families and students. We started small but grew by keeping our prices fair. Our mission is to always grow and make sure our food is available to everyone.

Our Vision

As we entered the 2000s, big fast-food places like KFC and Pizza Hut became popular. Instead of being scared, we saw it as a challenge. We want our bakery to become successful in the fast-food world. We keep changing and trying new things, and we also want to use the internet to reach more people and make our bakery really modern.
Breesy Bite Bakers

Since 1980, our bakery near Kandy has grown by offering quality and affordable products. By 1995, we had seven locations. In 2000, facing competition, we diversified and now focus on technology, creating a user-friendly website for younger consumers who prefer smartphones and the internet.
